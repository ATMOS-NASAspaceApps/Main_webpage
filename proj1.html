LUNAR LANDER-2 (LL-2) 2023

LUNAR LANDER 2023

LL-2 is the second lunar exploration mission developed by Chinmayaram Space Research Organisation (CSRO), after LL-1. It consists of a lunar orbiter, and also included the Galelio lander, and the Keplar lunar rover, all of which were developed in India. The main scientific objective is to map and study the variations in lunar surface composition, as well as the location and abundance of lunar water..

The spacecraft will launch on its mission to the Moon from the second launch pad at the Chinmayaram Space Centre in North Sentinal island 22 July 2023 at 09:13:12 UTC by Falcon Heavy rocket. The craft will reach the Moon's orbit on 20 August 2023 and began orbital positioning manoeuvres for the landing of the Galelio lander.

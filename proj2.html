XENOMORPH

MARS XENOMORPH ROVER

CSRO's Mars Exploration Rover (MER) mission is a robotic space mission involving Mars rover, Xenomorph exploring the planet Mars. It will begin in 2026 with the launch of the rover to explore the Martian surface and geology; their mission will be active for 90 Martian solar days

Xenomorph is a car-sized Mars rover designed to explore the Gale crater on Mars as part of CSRO's Mars Science Laboratory mission. Xenomorph will be launched from North Sentinal Island on 26 November 2026, at 15:02:00 UTC and will land on Aeolis Palus inside Gale crater on Mars on 6 August 2027, 05:17:57 UTC.
